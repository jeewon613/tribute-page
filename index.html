Get to know Gyeoungbokgung Palace

It was in 1395, three years after the Joseon Dynasty was founded by King Taejo, when the construction of the main royal Palace was completed after the capital of the newly founded dynasty moved from Gaeseong to Seoul. With Mount Bugaksan to its rear and Mount Namsan in the foreground, the site of Gyeongbokgung Palace was at the heart of Seoul and, indeed, deemed auspicious according to the traditional practice of geomancy.

Events You Can Participate!

Sluice Shift Ceremony


  • Time: Everyday(twice a day), 10:00 and 14:00
  • Place: In front of the Hyupsengmoon
  • Content: It is an event that reproduces the duty change of the officers in the Joseon Dynasty.

Palace Suragan Tasting


  • Time: Every Weekends of May, 19:00~21:00
  • Place: External Sojoo Room
  • Content: Experience royal cuisine (gongjung soldier and palace trolley) and watch traditional performances

Palace Night Performance


  • Time: 2019.09.06~10.27, 20:00~20:50
  • Place: Walldae in front of Sojungjun
  • Content: Korean traditional dance, pansori, Jeongga, and Gugak chamber music are available.
